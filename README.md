Productivity App
Overview

A simple productivity app to help you organize your tasks and notes. Includes a calendar to track your events and a notes section to jot down important information.
---
Features

Calendar

Add, view, and delete events.

Notes

Create, edit, and delete notes.
---
Technologies Used

HTML – Structure of the app

CSS – Styling and layout

JavaScript – Functionality and interactivity
---
Usage

Open the app in your browser.

Add an Event: Click on a date in the calendar and enter event details.
